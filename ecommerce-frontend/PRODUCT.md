# EcommAdmin

## Product Purpose
Admin dashboard for a Colombian e-commerce platform. Internal tool used by store operators to manage products, inventory, orders, users, payments, and shipping. Design SERVES the product — users are in task mode, not browsing.

## Register
product

## Users
Store operators and administrators in Colombia. Desktop-first. Users are comfortable with data-dense interfaces (think Notion, Linear, Stripe Dashboard). They need fast scanning, clear status at a glance, and efficient CRUD workflows.

## Brand Tone
Clean, professional, trustworthy. Efficient without being sterile. Not playful, not corporate-stiff. Like a well-built internal tool that someone clearly cared about.

## Anti-references
- Bootstrap admin templates (blue navbar, huge sidebar, bulky cards)
- Material Design (colorful chips, paper elevation, Google blue)
- Generic SaaS "AI-generated" look (purple-blue gradients, glassmorphism, gradient text metrics)
- Cluttered dashboards with 20+ widgets

## Color Strategy
Restrained. One slate/indigo accent. Neutral surface. The data is the focus, not the chrome.

## Key Surfaces
- Dashboard with KPI cards and order chart
- Data tables for all 10+ entities (Products, Users, Orders, Payments, Inventory, etc.)
- Create/Edit dialogs with validated forms
- Status badges (Order status, Payment status, Cart status, Movement type)
